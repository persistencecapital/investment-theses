2. PROTOCOL FUNDAMENTALS & TOKENOMICS

Arweave is a decentralised permanent storage network with unique tokenomics designed for long-term sustainability. AR token serves as both storage payment medium and mining incentive.

Metric Value Notes
Genesis Supply 55M AR June 8, 2018
Maximum Supply 66M AR Hard cap
Current Supply 65.65M AR 99.5% of max
Current Endowment ~150K AR Jan 2025 estimate
Emission Rate ~2% annually Exponential decay

Endowment Mechanism (Key Innovation): 80% of all storage fees are locked into the endowment, funding perpetual storage. Only 20% goes to miners immediately. This creates a deflationary sink that grows with network usage.

Historical Endowment Growth

  • Jan 2023: 44,000 AR

3. THE 2025 CROSSOVER.

For the first time since genesis (2019), the rate of AR entering the endowment (locked forever) exceeds the rate of new AR being mined.

2025 Annual Flows

  • New Mining Rewards: ~135,000 AR/year (declining)
  • Endowment Lockup: ~119,000 AR/year (growing)
  • Net New Supply: ~16,000 AR/year

Projection (2026-2030): By 2027, endowment lockup is projected to exceed 200,000 AR annually while new emissions fall below 100,000 AR. This will create a net negative flow of ~100,000 AR/year from circulating supply.

AR Token Supply Dynamics: New Emissions vs Endowment Lockup (2019-2035)

Unlike buyback-and-burn mechanisms, Arweave's endowment is protocol-enforced and grows automatically with usage. There is no governance risk or execution dependency. Every storage transaction permanently removes 80% of fees from circulation.

5. AO NETWORK: THE MULTIPLIER CATALYST

What is AO?

Launched February 2024, AO is Arweave's hyperparallel computing layer that enables smart contracts, social applications, and decentralised computation while using Arweave for permanent data storage.

The Flywheel Effect

AO Activity → Storage Demand → AR Fees → Endowment Lockup → Supply Reduction → Scarcity Premium

Every AO transaction:

  • Stores computation results on Arweave (permanent)
  • Pays storage fees in AR (80% to endowment)
  • Creates new use cases without inflating token supply

Post-AO Launch Impact (Feb 2024 - Oct 2025)

  • Social Media usage: 8.7% → 15.0% of total storage (+72%)
  • DApp data: 3.3% → 5.0% of total storage (+52%)
  • AI Training: 3.3% → 5.0% (+52%)
  • Total storage acceleration: 220 TiB → 336 TiB (+53% in 10 months)

Strategic Positioning: AO positions Arweave as both storage layer AND computing layer, competing with Ethereum L2s, Solana, and specialised DA layers. It does this while maintaining superior economics through permanent storage model, opposed to others that issue high token emissions to 'pay' users (and farmers) to use the product. The model proves product-market-fit where real customers are willing to pay to use the product.
